Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

General

How is BQ Technologies different from other dev agencies in Tanzania?

We focus on speed, quality, and affordability without cutting corners. We don't build fancy features nobody uses. For common needs (schools, microfinance, cashpoints, warehouses), we deploy ready-made systems in weeks, not months. For custom projects, we deliver clean, secure code your team can maintain long-term. Bottom line: you get production-grade software at a price that makes sense for Tanzania.

What can BQ Technologies build?

Three main areas: (1) Custom Software Development — web apps, mobile apps, integrations, systems tailored to your business. (2) Agentic AI Development — AI agents that work 24/7 (customer service, data analysis, process automation). (3) Cybersecurity — security audits, vulnerability analysis, system hardening, compliance consulting. We also offer ready-made systems: School Management, Microfinance Management, Cashpoint Management, and Warehouse Management — proven, battle-tested, deployed fast.

What's your competitive edge?

Speed, quality, affordability, and local advantage. We deploy production systems in 2–3 weeks. Everything is built for scale from day one. We understand Tanzania's business reality. We're not competing on price alone — we're solving real problems that cost businesses money.

Ready-Made Systems

What's the difference between a ready-made system and custom development?

Ready-made systems are pre-built, tested, and deployed in 2–3 weeks — perfect if your needs match standard workflows (schools, microfinance, etc.), with lower cost and faster deployment. Custom development is built specifically for you: it takes longer (4–8 weeks) and costs more, but solves unique problems. Use ready-made if your workflow is standard; use custom if you have special requirements.

Can we customize a ready-made system?

Yes. Ready-made systems are starting points, not restrictions. We can add custom fields and workflows, integrate with your existing tools, white-label it with your branding, and add the specific reports you need. Customizations add 1–2 weeks but cost far less than building from scratch.

How long does it take to deploy a ready-made system?

2–3 weeks from signing to live. Week 1: setup, data migration, training prep. Week 2: testing, adjustments, staff training. Week 3: go live, monitoring, fine-tuning. We don't do month-long implementations — we move fast.

Custom Development

How long does a custom project take?

Depends on scope. Small project (landing page, simple app): 2–3 weeks. Medium project (web app with 5–10 features): 4–8 weeks. Large project (complex system with integrations): 8–16 weeks. We'll give you an exact timeline after understanding your requirements. No surprises.

How much does development cost?

Every project is different. We work with you to understand what you actually need (not feature bloat), your budget, and timeline priorities. Then we give you a fixed quote with no surprises. Email us and we'll have a 30-minute call.

What's your development process?

(1) Discovery call (30 min) — understand your problem, not just what you think you need. (2) Requirements & proposal (3–5 days) — we write it down, you approve, we quote. (3) Development — we work in 2-week sprints, you see progress. (4) Testing & feedback — you test, we iterate. (5) Go live — deploy, monitor, support. You know what's happening at every step. No disappearing for 3 months.

Do you work with existing systems or APIs?

Yes. We can integrate with existing software (accounting, CRM, etc.), build APIs for your system, connect to third-party services (payment gateways, SMS, email), migrate data from old systems, and build mobile apps that talk to your backend. We're not locked into one tech stack — we use what works best for your problem.

AI & Automation

What kind of AI can you build?

We specialize in Agentic AI — systems that act autonomously. Customer service agents (answer questions 24/7, handle orders, refunds). Data analysis agents (summarize reports, find patterns, alert you to issues). Process automation agents (handle repetitive tasks like invoice processing and scheduling). Sales agents (qualify leads, send follow-ups, book meetings). These aren't chatbots that just answer questions — they're autonomous workers that do real business tasks.

How long does an AI agent take to build?

1.5–3 weeks depending on complexity. Most AI agents are live in 2 weeks because we're not building from scratch — we're training and integrating existing models.

How much does an AI agent cost?

Budget 500k–2M TZS for a production agent. That includes development, training, deployment, and 1 month of support.

Security

How do you approach cybersecurity?

We don't wait for breaches — we audit first. Our process: (1) Assessment — find vulnerabilities before attackers do. (2) Analysis — understand your risk (critical, medium, low). (3) Implementation — fix issues step by step. (4) Training — your team learns secure practices. (5) Monitoring — ongoing protection. We protect your data, your customers' data, and your reputation.

What does a security audit include?

Network penetration testing (can we break in?), code review (are there vulnerabilities in your software?), access control review (who has access to what?), compliance check (do you meet industry standards?), and security training for your team. Takes 2–3 weeks. Costs 500k–1M TZS depending on system size.

Support & Maintenance

What happens after we go live?

We provide 30 days of free support (bug fixes, small adjustments), training so your team learns the system, and documentation covering usage and common troubleshooting. After that, we offer optional maintenance plans (monthly fee) for ongoing support.

What if something breaks after launch?

We fix it fast. Your system has monitoring — we often know about issues before you do. Critical issues: fixed same day. Non-critical: fixed within 48 hours. You're never abandoned.

Do you provide training?

Yes, training is included: live sessions where we walk your team through the system, video documentation you can reference anytime, and ongoing support with questions answered quickly. Your team ends up confident, not confused.

How do I start a project with BQ Technologies?

No. We work on fixed-price projects (you pay for scope, we deliver), time & materials for ongoing work (you pay for hours, tracked transparently), and optional maintenance contracts (monthly fee for ongoing support). No lock-in — we keep you happy so you keep us around.

Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: 5 July 2026

1. Introduction

BQ Technologies ("we", "us", "our") operates the website bqtech.co.tz. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep it simple: we collect as little as possible, and we never sell your data.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information you choose to give us:

  • Contact details — your name, email address, and message when you contact us by email, phone, WhatsApp, or through our contact form.
  • Newsletter email — your email address if you subscribe to our updates.
  • Basic usage data — general, anonymous statistics about site visits (such as pages viewed and device type) to help us improve the website.

We do not collect payment information, ID numbers, or any sensitive personal data through this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide quotes.
  • To deliver our services if you become a client.
  • To send occasional tips and updates if you subscribed to our newsletter.
  • To understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Messages you send us are stored in our business email account, protected by industry-standard security. We keep your information only as long as needed to respond to you or serve you as a client. As a company that offers cybersecurity services, we apply the same security standards to our own data that we recommend to our clients.

5. Cookies

This website uses minimal browser storage: we remember your dark/light theme preference on your own device. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. If we add analytics, it will only measure anonymous, aggregate usage.

6. Your Rights

You can, at any time:

  • Ask what information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete your information.
  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter with one message.

Just contact us using the details below and we will act on your request promptly.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website links to third-party platforms (such as WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube). Those platforms have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
